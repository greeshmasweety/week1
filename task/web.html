Special Characters and Symbols

  • © - The copyright symbol. It is used to indicate that the content is protected by copyright law.
  • < - The less-than symbol. It is used to compare two values in mathematical and programming contexts.
  • > - The greater-than symbol. It is used to compare two values in mathematical and programming contexts.
  • & - The ampersand symbol. It is used to represent the word "and" and is commonly used in programming and HTML.
  • € - The Euro currency symbol. It is used to represent the Euro, the official currency of the Eurozone.
  • ™ - The trademark symbol. It is used to indicate that a word, phrase, or symbol is a registered trademark.
